About Us

Find grocery shopping too much of a hassle?Trying to find a quick bite,but too lazy to go out shopping? Fret not,because "3verdays"is here to save the day! "3verdays" is an online shopping website which is comprised of many different product categories that makes it an excellent chocie for a one-stop shop for your everyday needs.

This gives the user much needed convenience when shopping online and also reduces time used for surveying other websites for various kinds of daily products that the user desires. Hence, our slogan "Everything you like, Everything you need, Everything you want" which is stated in the number "3" in our logo. In addition to the website offering a plethora of product choices,the user interface has also been streamlined to prioritise user easy access to different categories within the website. An account login option has also been added to tailor the website to different people's needs.

HTML, CSS and Javascripts languages had been used in the process of web development.
